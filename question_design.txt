R = required, O = optional
OE = open-ended, MC = multiple-choice (choose only one), CATA = check all that apply, NUM = give a number between (0 or 1) and X
[Answer or number choices for MC, CATA, or NUM are in brackets]
For MC and CATA questions that has "Other" as an option, the user should be able to select it and type their answer in.

Option A: The user doesn't know where they want to go

1 (R, MC). Do you have any idea what you want to do or what types of places you want to go? [Yes, No]
    2 (R, OE). (Only if answer is "yes" to previous question.) Describe the type(s) of places you want to go. The more specific, the better. For example, you could say "I want to go to a place (or places) where there are lots of hiking trails and beaches."
3 (R, MC). Do you know how long you want your trip to be? [Yes, No]
    4 (R, NUM). (Only if answer is "yes" to previous question.) How long is your trip? Enter the number of days. [1-30]
5 (R, MC). How many people are planning to go on your trip, including yourself? [1, 2, 3-4, 5-6, 7-9, 10-14, 15 or more]
6 (R, MC). How are you planning on getting to your destination? [Driving, Flying, Train, Bus, Cruise or ferry, Not sure yet]
7 (R, OE). Where are you currently? If possible, include the city and country.
8 (R, MC). Do you want to travel to a different country? Note: If you don't have a valid passport that allows international travel, answer "no" to this question. [Yes, No]
    9 (R, OE). (Only if answer is "yes" to previous question.) What country (or countries) do you prefer? Only list countries where your passport is valid for travel.
    10 (R, MC). (Only if answer is "no" to Q8.) How far do you want your destination to be away from your current location? [<10 miles, 10-20 miles, 20-50 miles, 50-100 miles, 100-200 miles, 200-500 miles, >500 miles]
11 (R, MC). Do you know the dates that you want your trip to be? [Yes, No]
    12 (R, OE). (Only if answer is "yes" to previous question.) To make the AI understand the dates as clear as possible, enter the dates here in this format: [Month] [Day] to [Month] [Day].
13 (R, MC). Are there any strict time constraints on specific days? [Yes, No]
    14 (R, OE). (Only if answer is "yes" to previous question.) To make the AI understand the dates and times as clear as possible, enter the day(s) and time(s) you have time constraints in this format: [Month] [Day] from [hh:mm] [AM/PM] to [hh:mm] [AM/PM]. To enter more than one date or time, use commas to separate them.
15 (R, MC). Are you planning to visit one area (like one city) or multiple areas (an example would be a trip across the entire Oregon Coast)? [Yes, No]
16 (R, CATA). Are you traveling with children, disabled people, and/or elderly people? Check all that apply. [Children, Disabled People, Elderly People, None]
17 (R, MC). Do you (or anyone in your group) have any mobility limitations and/or accessibility needs? [Yes, No]
    18 (R, OE). (Only if answer is "yes" to previous question.) Explain the mobility limitations and/or accessibility needs here.
19 (O, MC). Is money a concern? [Yes, No]
    20 (O, NUM). (Only if answer is "yes" to previous question.) How much are you (or your group, if you are going with at least one person) willing to spend? If you're not sure, feel free to leave this blank. [$1-$100,000]
21 (O, CATA). What types of weather do you want to avoid or can't tolerate? [Extreme heat, Extreme cold, Heavy rain, Snow / icy conditions, High humidity, Low humidity, Strong wind, Hurricane / storm season, None — weather doesn’t matter much, Not sure yet]
22 (O, OE). What types of things interest you?
23 (O, NUM). Are you into restaurants and food? Rate on a scale from 1-10 (1 = not at all, 10 = very much). [1-10]
24 (O, CATA). What types of cuisine do you like? If you aren't really that much into restaurants and food, feel free to skip this question. [American, Chinese, French, Greek, Indian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Mediterranean, Mexican, Middle Eastern, Spanish, Thai, Vietnamese, Other]
25 (O, NUM). Are you into shops, shopping malls, and buying stuff? Rate on a scale from 1-10 (1 = not at all, 10 = very much). [1-10]
26 (O, CATA). What types of things are you interested in buying? If you aren't really that much into buying stuff, feel free to skip this question. [Antiques, Art, Books, Clothing / fashion, Electronics, Handmade crafts, Jewelry, Local specialty foods, Luxury goods, Outdoor gear, Souvenirs, Street markets, Vintage items, Other]
27 (O, OE). What is the main purpose of this trip?
28 (O, MC). Do you prefer a packed schedule or a relaxed one? [Packed, Relaxed, Somewhere in between]
29 (O, OE). What are some things you definitely want to do?
30 (O, OE). What are some things you definitely want to avoid?
31 (O, NUM). How physically active do you want this trip to be? Rate on a scale from 1-10 (1 = not at all, 10 = very much). [1-10]
32 (O, NUM). How comfortable are you with using public transportation? Rate on a scale from 1-10 (1 = not at all, 10 = very much). [1-10]
33 (O, MC). Do you want nightlife included? [Yes, No]
34 (O, NUM). How important is photography or Instagram-style spots? Rate on a scale from 1-10 (1 = not at all, 10 = very much). [1-10]
35 (O, OE). How do you want to feel at the end of this trip?
36 (O, MC). Would you rather spend more time traveling or more time in fewer places? [More time traveling, more time in fewer places, Somewhere in between]
37 (O, OE). Anywhere (cities, districts, important attractions) that you don't want to visit (or don't have time to visit)?
38 (O, MC). When do you want to start exploring each day (or most days)? [7 AM, 8 AM, 9 AM, 10 AM, 11 AM, 12 PM, Other]
39 (O, MC). When do you want to go back to your hotel (or the place you're staying) each day (or most days)? [5 PM, 6 PM, 7 PM, 8 PM, 9 PM, 10 PM, 11 PM, 12 AM, Other]
40 (O, OE). Anything else that you want to add? The more specific, the better.
